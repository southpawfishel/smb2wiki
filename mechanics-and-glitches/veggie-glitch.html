The Veggie Glitch is a very precise trick that allows the player to carry an item through a door for use on another screen. The steps to perform the glitch are as follows:

  1. Pick up a potion.
  2. Throw the potion such that the subspace door is on top of a veggie.
  3. Enter subspace, then immediately exit subspace.
  4. On the first frame after exiting subspace, press B to pick up the veggie. If timed correctly, the veggie will be "stuck" inside the character's head rather than carried above the character's head. After walking through a door, the character will be carrying the veggie normally.

The best-known application of this glitch is to carry a veggie into the Birdo screen on 1-2 to kill Birdo more quickly. This glitch can also be used to pick up a POW block, which is used to perform the most advanced version of the Ladder Clip in 1-1.
