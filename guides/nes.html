Any%

Rules

  1. Timing begins when you press start at the beginning of the game (before character select).
  2. Timing ends when you enter the door after defeating Wart.
  3. Warps may be used to skip levels.

Route

When trying to finish the game as fast as possible, you want to use Toad in most of the levels due to his fast run speed while holding an item, though Luigi is used in a few levels due to his high jumping and slow fall speed. Warps are used to get from 1-3 to 4-1 and from 4-2 to 6-1.

  1. 1-1 (Toad)
  2. 1-2 (Toad)
  3. 1-3 (Toad)
  4. 4-1 (Toad)
  5. 4-2 (Toad)
  6. 6-1 (Toad)
  7. 6-2 (Luigi)
  8. 6-3 (Luigi)
  9. 7-1 (Luigi)
  10. 7-2 (Toad)
